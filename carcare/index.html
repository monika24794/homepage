Services

Interior Deep Cleaning

In the Interior deep cleaning service we use car cleaners which are specially made for the interior. With the help of these cleaners we clean each and every corner of your car interior which includes seats cleaning, upholstery cleaning, carpet cleaning, roof cleaning, dashboard and door panels cleaning etc.

More

Antibacterial 2-Step Cleaning

In this service all the points which come in the deep cleaning are covered as well as in the second step we use a special 3M car Interior foam for antibacterial cleaning.

Car Polishing

In the car polishing service we start with hard water removal cleaning, tar spot removal process, alloy wheels cleaning, sometimes clay bar cleaning followed by buffing and polishing for swirl marks removal and high gloss using the best in class dual action polisher machine.

Car Ceramic Or Graphene Coating

In this service we do complete car exterior detailing with use of 3 step polishing action. It is a more conventional method with the use of a rotary machine. Additional polymer coating, 9H Ceramic coating or Paint Protection Film can be opted after this service for more protection.

Car PPF Coating

In this service we do complete car exterior detailing with use of 3 step polishing action. It is a more conventional method with the use of a rotary machine. Additional polymer coating, 9H Ceramic coating or Paint Protection Film can be opted after this service for more protection.

Denting and painting
